## Dormitory Rules Story

`宿舍规则怪谈：熄灯后，请勿点名` uses the same selected Taira Komori library already packaged with the project. Its active cues are deliberately sparse: room/corridor/archive beds, broadcast-start signal, two semantically different knock patterns, phone-video handling, paper, keys, console signal, and final-record tension. Red-light transitions and unverified actions remain silent rather than using a near-match sound.

The public-rules broadcast and final corrected-rollcall broadcast are **not yet represented by a voice asset**. They are explicitly held for an authorised, human-performed Chinese recording with a calm institutional delivery and modest public-speaker treatment. The runtime does not use browser TTS, generated fallback voice, or a generic character clip for either cue.
